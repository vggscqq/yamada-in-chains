**Privacy Policy**

This bot collects and stores the text of messages sent in chats where learning is enabled, along with your Telegram user ID, solely for the purpose of training its Markov chain model.

**What is stored:**
- Your Telegram user ID
- The text of messages you send in enabled chats

**What is NOT stored:**
- Your name, username, or profile photo
- Any media (photos, videos, stickers, etc.)

**Your rights:**
- You may revoke consent at any time via /manageconsent
- After revoking consent, new messages will not be stored
- You may delete all your stored messages with /deleteme confirm (works in private chat only)
- Group admins may remove your messages from a session with /deletefrom (reply to your message)

**Data retention:**
- Messages are stored indefinitely unless deleted by you or a group admin
- Deleting a session deletes all messages within it

By sending messages in a chat where this bot is enabled, you consent to the above.
